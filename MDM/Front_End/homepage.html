About MATHION

MATHION was created to address a critical gap in educational accessibility. While digital learning has advanced rapidly, students with physical disabilities often face significant barriers when working with mathematical formulas and equations. Traditional input methods can be impossible for those with limited mobility.

Our team of accessibility experts, mathematics educators, and developers collaborated with students with disabilities to create a solution that leverages the spatial computing capabilities of Apple Vision Pro. By integrating eye-tracking, gesture recognition, and voice commands, we've developed an intuitive system that allows students to create complex LaTeX formulas without traditional keyboard and mouse inputs.

We believe that all students deserve equal access to quality mathematics education. MATHION represents our commitment to making the abstract language of mathematics accessible to everyone, regardless of physical ability.
